
# Online Shopping Management System

An online shopping management system, often referred to as an e-commerce system, is a software application that facilitates the buying and selling of products or services over the internet. These systems are designed to streamline and automate various aspects of online retail operations. The success of an online shopping management system depends on its usability, security, and the quality of the products or services offered. Additionally, ongoing maintenance and optimization are crucial for long-term success in the highly competitive e-commerce market.

The folowing code is a full stack code using html, css, bootstrap, js, jquery, ajax, php and mysql. And the project is also a progressive web app which means you can download or install the application into your smart phones, tablets and desktops.
